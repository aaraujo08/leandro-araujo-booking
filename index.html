My story

From Copacabana to County Dublin.

I grew up in Rio de Janeiro, where gardens grow themselves — sun, rain, repeat. In 2014 I packed a bag, landed in Ireland, and the first thing I noticed wasn't the cold. It was how impossibly green everything was.

Ireland taught me a different kind of gardening. Patience. Reading the sky. Knowing that the same hedge will surprise you every season. I bring the soft hands my grandmother taught me in Petrópolis and the precision Irish weather demands.

Today I look after homes from Rathmines to Bray — one client at a time, every visit personal.
